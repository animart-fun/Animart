Privacy Policy

Animart keeps privacy expectations simple for this storefront demo: we collect the minimum information needed to support sign-in, cart, checkout, and support interactions inside your browser.

Information We Store

Account details, cart contents, selected checkout items, and contact form submissions are stored locally in your browser for demo purposes. This helps the storefront remember what you selected without requiring a backend.

How We Use Information

  • To keep you signed in across pages on this device.
  • To remember cart selections, checkout items, and saved support messages.
  • To improve browsing flow and personalize the account greeting in the header.

Your Control

You can clear locally stored data at any time by signing out or removing site data from the browser. Because this version runs without a live backend, nothing is transmitted to external servers as part of the demo flow.
