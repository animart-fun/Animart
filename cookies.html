Cookie Policy

Animart relies on browser storage in place of server-side sessions for this demo storefront. This policy explains what is remembered and why.

What We Remember

  • Signed-in user details for the current device.
  • Saved cart items, checkout choices, and selected products.
  • Support messages submitted through the Contact Us form.

Why It Matters

Remembering this information lets the site keep your shopping flow intact between pages. It helps product search, cart selection, and checkout feel more realistic without requiring a backend service.

Managing Storage

You can clear browser storage at any time to remove remembered account details, cart items, or contact form entries. Doing so resets the storefront demo back to its default state on your device.
